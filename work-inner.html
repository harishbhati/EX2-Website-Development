Coca Cola / brand recognition

Coca-Cola is an American multinational beverage corporation, manufacturer, retailer, and marketer of nonalcoholic beverage concentrates and syrups. The company now produces 3,500+ different beverages in numerous categories, all sold around the world.We collaborated with Coca-Cola to develop a mobile application for their Employee Ambassador Program. The program’s goal was to empower its 700,000+ employees worldwide to represent and promote the Coca-Cola Brand.

In addition to improving brand recognition and POS presentation, our mobile needed to:

  • Improve the presentation of their products at point of sale in stores and supermarkets to increase product sales.
  • Ensure ease-of-use, and thus greater employee adoption, of the Ambassador Program.
  • Map both points of sale and areas of distribution that were being the most negatively affected by poor presentation, so Coke new where to direct their resources.
  • Eliminate the need for printed sheets used by management when moving employees through the training process.

Given our development success and employee adoption, Coca-Cola saw great improvments.
